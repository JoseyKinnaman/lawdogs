A page about the dogs of law enforcement. At all levels.

Law dogs through the eyes of a civilian dog.

Most people, when it comes to where dogs and the law intersect, only think of K9 officers.

This is certainly not the case. Dogs help enforce, interpret, and challenge the law at all levels. For instance, there are:

  • K9 officers, obviously
  • Dog lawyers
  • Dog judges

There are also dogs on the other side of the law, which law dogs work especially hard to bring to justice.
